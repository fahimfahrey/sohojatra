# How to Install Sohojatra as a PWA

## What is a PWA?

A Progressive Web App (PWA) lets you install Sohojatra on your device like a native app, without going through an app store. You get faster loading, offline access, and a full-screen experience.

## Installation Instructions

### 📱 On Android (Chrome/Samsung Internet)

#### Method 1: Using the Install Prompt

1. Open Sohojatra in your browser
2. Wait 3 seconds for the install banner to appear at the bottom
3. Tap **"Install"**
4. Confirm the installation
5. The app will be added to your home screen

#### Method 2: Using Browser Menu

1. Open Sohojatra in Chrome
2. Tap the **three dots** (⋮) in the top-right corner
3. Select **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
4. Tap **"Install"** or **"Add"**
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen

### 📱 On iPhone/iPad (Safari - iOS 16.4+)

1. Open Sohojatra in Safari
2. Tap the **Share button** (square with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Edit the name if desired
5. Tap **"Add"** in the top-right corner
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen

### 💻 On Desktop (Chrome/Edge)

#### Method 1: Using the Install Button

1. Open Sohojatra in Chrome or Edge
2. Look for the **install icon** (⊕ or computer icon) in the address bar
3. Click the icon
4. Click **"Install"** in the popup
5. The app will open in its own window

#### Method 2: Using Browser Menu

1. Open Sohojatra in Chrome or Edge
2. Click the **three dots** (⋮) in the top-right corner
3. Select **"Install Sohojatra"** or **"Apps" → "Install this site as an app"**
4. Click **"Install"**
5. The app will open in its own window

## After Installation

### Launching the App

- **Mobile**: Tap the Sohojatra icon on your home screen
- **Desktop**: Find Sohojatra in your applications/start menu, or click the icon in your taskbar

### App Features

✅ **Faster Loading** - Cached assets load instantly  
✅ **Offline Access** - View cached content without internet  
✅ **Full Screen** - No browser UI, just the app  
✅ **Push Notifications** - Get ride updates even when the app is closed  
✅ **Auto Updates** - Always get the latest version automatically

### Uninstalling the App

#### On Android:

1. Long-press the Sohojatra icon
2. Select **"Uninstall"** or drag to **"Remove"**
3. Or go to Settings → Apps → Sohojatra → Uninstall

#### On iPhone/iPad:

1. Long-press the Sohojatra icon
2. Tap **"Remove App"**
3. Select **"Delete App"**

#### On Desktop:

1. Open the app
2. Click the **three dots** (⋮) in the title bar
3. Select **"Uninstall Sohojatra"**
4. Or go to browser settings → Apps → Manage apps → Uninstall

## Troubleshooting

### Install Option Not Showing?

- Make sure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari 16.4+)
- Check that you're on HTTPS (not HTTP)
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache and try again

### App Not Working Offline?

- Make sure you've opened the app at least once while online
- Check that service workers are enabled in your browser
- Try reinstalling the app

### Updates Not Appearing?

- Close and reopen the app
- The app updates automatically in the background
- Force refresh: Clear app data and reopen

## Need Help?

If you encounter any issues with installation or usage, please contact support or check the browser console for error messages.
